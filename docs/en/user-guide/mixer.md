# Mixer

> Features to adjust the overall project volume (master volume).

## Overview
The mixer panel is used to manage the final volume level of the audio output. It is located at the bottom of the screen alongside the transport controls as the "Mixer", allowing you to operate the master channel's volume (Master Volume).

*Note: Individual track mixing features such as volume and panning are adjusted in the **Track Details** panel, which appears on the right side of the screen when a track is selected.*

## Basic Usage
### Master Controls
1. Drag the **Master Volume** slider left or right to adjust the overall output volume.

## Detailed Settings
The mixer panel manipulates the following parameters.

### Master Channel
| Parameter | Description | Default Value | Range |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Master Volume | The final output volume of the entire project | 80 | 0 to 100 |

## Keyboard Shortcuts
In the current version, there are no keyboard shortcuts dedicated to mixer operations.
