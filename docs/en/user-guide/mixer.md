# Mixer

> A panel for adjusting the volume of individual tracks and controlling the master output.

## Overview
The **Mixer & Effects** panel is used to adjust the final overall output volume of your project and balance individual tracks.
In the current version, basic volume adjustment and a mute function are implemented for the master output.

## Basic Usage
1. Locate the **Mixer & Effects** panel at the bottom of the screen.
2. Drag the **Master Volume** slider left or right to adjust the overall output volume.
3. Click the speaker icon (🔊 / 🔇) next to the slider to toggle the mute state on or off.

## Detailed Settings
The currently implemented parameters are as follows:

| Parameter | Description | Default | Range |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Master Volume | The final overall output volume of the project | 0.8 | 0.0 - 1.0 |
| Mute | The mute (silence) state of the master output | Off | On / Off |
