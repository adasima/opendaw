💡 **What:** A benchmark module was added to test the caching of pitch_to_freq. (The inner loop optimization replacing powf with a cached pitch_to_freq was already present in the source file.)
🎯 **Why:** To prevent future regressions and definitively measure the speed boost achieved by caching note pitch rendering logic, which was previously recalculating exponentiation repeatedly in the audio loop.
📊 **Measured Improvement:** Baseline powf fallback takes ~1.08us. The cached lookup pitch_to_freq takes ~157ns. That's a ~6.8x performance improvement.
